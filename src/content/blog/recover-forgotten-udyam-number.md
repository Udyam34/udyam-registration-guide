Misplacing your **Udyam Registration Number (URN)** is common — it often isn't saved properly after registration, or the person who registered the business has since moved on. The good news: you don't need to register again. You can recover a forgotten Udyam number online in a few minutes.

## What you'll need

- The **mobile number or email** registered during your Udyam registration (a one-time password is sent there).
- Your business and owner details as entered during registration, in case they're needed for verification.

## Steps to recover your Udyam number

1. Go to the official Udyam portal and open the **"Forgot Udyam / URN"** option under the registration menu.
2. Choose how to receive the **OTP** — on your registered mobile or registered email.
3. Enter the OTP to verify, and your **Udyam Registration Number** will be displayed.

If you no longer have access to the registered mobile or email, those details must be updated first, since recovery relies on OTP verification. In that case it's easiest to [recover your Udyam number](https://www.udyam-registration.com/forgot-udyam/) through a guided service that handles the verification for you.

## After you've recovered the number

Once you have your URN back, you can [print your Udyam certificate](https://www.udyam-registration.com/print-udyam-certificate/) straight away, or update any business details that have changed.

## FAQ

**Do I have to pay to recover my Udyam number?** No — retrieving a forgotten URN on the official portal is free.

**Can I recover it without my registered mobile/email?** Not directly — you'd first update the registered contact details, since recovery depends on OTP verification.

**Is my Udyam number the same as my old Udyog Aadhaar number?** If you migrated from Udyog Aadhaar, you were issued a new Udyam Registration Number — recover the Udyam (URN) version for current use.
